# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Maintainer Gate exists to reduce review pressure without turning contributors into targets. We expect contributors and maintainers to be direct, kind, and specific.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss behavior and evidence, not personal intent.
- Explain false positives with minimal reproducible examples.
- Keep issue threads focused on maintainers, pull requests, policy, and tooling.
- Assume good intent while still protecting maintainer time.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publicly accusing a contributor of using AI without evidence.
- Posting private information or real secrets.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or repeated off-topic comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to being blocked from the project.
